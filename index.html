All your coffee cards, one app.

Stamped is a digital loyalty app for specialty cafes. Collect stamps, earn rewards, and leave the paper stack at home.

Privacy policy Terms of service

For customers

Carry every participating cafe’s loyalty card on your phone. Scan a cafe QR to join, or show your code so staff can stamp your visit.

For cafes

Owners configure brand, locations, and cards; baristas use the same app to scan and redeem. Questions? Email hello@umair.au.
